Degree Program

Details

Digital Forensics Research and Service Center (DFRSC) under the umbrella of Lahore Garrison University (LGU) has taken this initiative of creating cyber security and digital forensics experts by introducing degree in specialized area of information technology. Since there no other university in Pakistan is offering these specialized degree program LGU will be the pioneer to offer such unique degree program in Pakistan.

DFRSC has designed first of its kind degree program in Pakistan BS Digital Forensic and Cyber Security. Overwhelming response is received for both batches and still we are receiving admissions.
