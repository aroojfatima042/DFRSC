Digital Forensics Research Service Center

Digital Forensic Research and Service Center (DFRSC) was established in June 2016 as subsidiary of Lahore Garrison University.

Despite being a relatively new university, LGU has made a remarkable impact on the canvas of higher education in Lahore by immensely progressing in all dimensions. In pursuance of the same endeavor various initiatives involving state of the art technologies and techniques have been adopted. Vice chancellor of LGU, Major General (R) Obaid bin Zakria envisions it to be the one of the leading innovative information technology education provider in the region, establishment of the DFRSC is important milestone toward achieving this goal.

It is the very first institute of its kind in Pakistan and surrounding countries. DFRSC offers various degree programs, and certificate courses in digital forensics, cyber security and white collar crime. It also offers services like recovery, verification analysis and reporting of digital evidence for presecution. Alongwith this, it has also conducted seminars, conferences, exhibitions and pulished research papers and articles in leading publications. There are almost 185 recognized universities in Pakistan. Most of their websites are vulnerable. Once a site is compromised it can be used for criminal activities. They can also be used for cyber terrorism and for recuirement purposes. The university management remains oblivious of the fact that their site is hacked, cyber criminals are discreet. DFRSC will analyze all the sites and will publish it in a report form. Our campus security and DNS security trainings will ensure that the administrators follow the procedures to keep their infrastructure safe.
