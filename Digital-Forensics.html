Digital Forensics

Digital Forensic Research and Service Center (DFRSC) was established in June 2016 as subsidiary of Lahore Garrison University. Despite being a relatively new university, LGU has made a remarkable impact on the canvas of higher education in Lahore by immensely progressing in all dimensions. In pursuance of the same endeavor various initiatives involving state of the art technologies and techniques have been adopted. Vice chancellor of LGU, Major General (R) Obaid bin Zakria envisions it to be the one of the leading innovative information technology education provider in the region, establishment of the DFRSC is important milestone toward achieving this goal.

What is Digital Forensics ?

Digital forensics is the process of collecting, preserving, analys- ing and presenting of digital evidence. This technology is usable for internal disciplinary Hearing, supporting data for internal incident reports and assisting other investigations as well. Digital Evidence is data which helps reconstruct past events or activity. It can show possession/handling of digital data, use/ abuse of IT infrastructure & services, evidence of organizational security policy and violation or illegal Activity.

DFRSC and Digital Forensics

It has established a state of the art Digital Forensics laboratory to provide service to public and private sector. It is the first research and traning facility centre of its kind in Pakistan and intends to extend its services to the region. In 2017, DFRSC established a Cyber Security Research and Service Center and a CERT is in the process of establishment, which will provide technology and services at national level. This centre will have a very specific purpose of creating awareness about Cyber Security and conducting security audits. It will conduct several trainings and offer degrees in Cyber Security In 2018, DFRSC established the International Institute of White Collar Crime Investigation which provides trainings, various investigative services to the public and private sector. The institute was inaugurated by the then Chief Justice of Pakistan, Mr. Justice Mian Saqib Nisar on June 2, 2018. Since its establishment, the of International Institute of White Collar Crime Investigation, has conducted sevral high profile investigations and provided services on to various organization like Supreme Court of Pakistan, Lahore High Court, LDA, Anti Corruption Establish Punjab, Federal Investigation Agency, Prosecutor General Punjab etc.. DFRSC also publishes a quarterly Journal called International of Electronic Crime Investigation.

Our Mission

  • To provide a quality education in science and practices of digital forensics, and to prepare the students for digital forensics jobs and lifetime of learning.
  • To provide digital forensics related services to law enforcement agencies, defense attorneys, corporations, financial intermediaries and those who want to acquire these services for legal purpose
  • To publish research papers, surveys, opinions and articles on the ongoing issues related to digital forensics in the region.
  • To develop various solutions and assist law enforcement agencies in the region in solving crimes.
  • To create awareness about the emerging technologies in digital forensics.

Main Areas

  • Computer forensics (hard disk, removable media acquisition and analysis)
  • Network forensics (network intrusions, abuse, etc.)
  • Software forensics (examining malicious code, malware, etc.)
  • Live system forensics (compromised hosts, system abuse, etc.)
  • Mobile forensics
